About

Join us for a groundbreaking 24-hour online webinar on December 14-15, hosted via Zoom. This event will delve into the complexities of “What is a Multiscale Human” a revolutionary research focus led by CNS in partnership with CIFAR.


Starting at 12:00 PM on December 14 and running until 12:00 PM on December 15, each hour will introduce a unique topic related to the multiscale mapping of the human body. This webinar offers a rare opportunity to dive into topics such as 3D visualization, human physiology, and the role of illustrations and animations in understanding complex biological systems. You’ll also learn about the program's founders, funding avenues, and the collaborative efforts advancing this research.
